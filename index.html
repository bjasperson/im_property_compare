Exploring Correlations Between Predicted Properties from Interatomic Models

Shared repositories for molecular simulation data present an opportunity to reinvent material science and mechanics research. Researchers can now probe numerous models and their predicted properties with ease. This opens up the possibility of using ensembles to aggregate and estimate property values.

However, it is important to understand the nuances that define the data that we leverage. As an example, we explore the observed correlations between elastic constants (C44 in particular) and surface energy.
